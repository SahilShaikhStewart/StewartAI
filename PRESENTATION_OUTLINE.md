## 🎯 Presentation Tips

### Before You Start:
1. **Practice 3+ times** - Know your timing
2. **Test live demo** - Ensure platform is responsive
3. **Prepare backup** - Screenshots if demo fails
4. **Know your audience** - Judges' backgrounds
5. **Anticipate questions** - Prepare answers

### During Presentation:
1. **Start strong** - Hook them in 30 seconds
2. **Show, don't tell** - Live demo is key
3. **Be confident** - You built something real
4. **Manage time** - 5 minutes goes fast
5. **End with impact** - Clear call to action

### Body Language:
- Stand tall, shoulders back
- Make eye contact with all judges
- Use hand gestures for emphasis
- Smile and show enthusiasm
- Move purposefully, don't pace

### Voice:
- Speak clearly and at moderate pace
- Vary tone to maintain interest
- Pause for emphasis
- Project confidence
- Avoid filler words (um, uh, like)
