## 🎯 Purpose
This document provides curated questions to ask the AI Knowledge Assistant during your demo, along with expected response patterns. These questions showcase different capabilities and use cases.

## 📚 Category 1: Basic Policy Queries (Easy Wins)

### Question 1: Easement Requirements
**Ask:**
```
What are the requirements for insuring a property with an easement?
```

**Expected Response Pattern:**
- Definition of easements in title insurance context
- Types of easements (appurtenant, in gross, prescriptive)
- Documentation requirements (recorded easement agreements, surveys)
- Underwriting considerations (impact on marketability, access rights)
- Source citations from policy documents

**Why This Works:**
- Demonstrates basic knowledge retrieval
- Shows source citation capability
- Common real-world scenario

---

### Question 2: Lien Priority
**Ask:**

**Expected Response Pattern:**

- Source citations

## 🎭 Demo Strategy Tips

### For Maximum Impact:

1. **Start Easy** - Begin with Question 1 or 2 to build confidence
2. **Show Complexity** - Move to Question 4 or 5 to demonstrate intelligence
3. **Highlight Comparison** - Use Question 7 or 8 to show analytical capability
4. **Test Boundaries** - Use Question 9 or 10 to show safety features
5. **End Practical** - Finish with Question 11 or 12 to show daily value

### Timing:
- **2 questions in 1.5 minutes** = 45 seconds per question
- Allow time for response to appear
- Point out key features while AI is "thinking"

### What to Highlight:
- ✅ **Source Citations** - "Notice it references specific policy documents"
- ✅ **Structured Responses** - "See how it organizes information clearly"
- ✅ **Actionable Guidance** - "It provides concrete next steps"
- ✅ **Safety Features** - "When it doesn't know, it says so"
- ✅ **Speed** - "This would take 15 minutes of manual research"
