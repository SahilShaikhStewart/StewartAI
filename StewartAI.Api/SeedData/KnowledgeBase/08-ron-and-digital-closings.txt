WHAT IS REMOTE ONLINE NOTARIZATION (RON)?
Remote Online Notarization (RON) is a technology-enabled process that allows notarizations to be completed entirely online via secure audio-video communication. The signer and notary connect through a digital platform, where identity verification, document review, and electronic signing all occur in real time.

Stewart's Signer's Choice platform is a next-generation notarization platform that brings together everything title companies, lenders, notaries, and signers need to complete real estate closings with confidence. Built for today's digital-first world, the platform supports both Remote Online Notarization (RON) and Mobile Notary services, giving clients the flexibility to close anytime, anywhere.

RON LEGAL FRAMEWORK
- RON is legally recognized in 40+ states as of 2024, with more states adopting annually
- Federal recognition under E-SIGN Act and UETA (Uniform Electronic Transactions Act)
- Interstate validity: RON documents are valid across state lines
- Court acceptance: Widely accepted in legal proceedings
- MISMO certified RON adherence for mortgage industry standards
- Notary jurisdiction is determined by where the notary is commissioned
- Signer can be located anywhere RON is not prohibited

WHEN TO USE RON VS MOBILE NOTARIZATION
Use RON when:
- Signers have a reliable internet connection (10+ Mbps recommended)
- Signers have a computer or tablet with camera and microphone
- Signers are in a quiet, private location
- Quick turnaround is needed (same-day availability possible)
- Signers are in two separate locations
- Real estate closings: Available 7 AM – 11 PM Eastern, 7 days a week

Use Mobile Notarization when:
- Signers have limited technology access
- Documents require wet (ink) signatures
- Underwriter or lender requires wet-signed documents
- Geographic coverage in major metropolitan areas
- Advance scheduling recommended (48-72 hours)

RON SESSION REQUIREMENTS
Technology Requirements:
- HD webcam or device camera
- Built-in or external microphone
- Stable broadband connection (10+ Mbps recommended)
- Latest version of Chrome browser with camera/microphone permissions
- Government-issued photo identification

Identity Verification for RON:
- Multi-factor verification: ID examination plus knowledge-based authentication (KBA) questions
- Biometric verification: Facial recognition and liveness detection
- Document authentication: ID authenticity verification
- Behavior analysis: Monitoring for suspicious activity patterns

RON SESSION TIMELINE
- Request Submission: Immediate
- Scheduler Contact: 2-4 hours
- Session Scheduling: Same day to 48 hours
- Session Completion: 20-60 minutes
- Document Delivery: Immediate (electronic)

MOBILE NOTARIZATION TIMELINE
- Request Submission: Immediate
- Scheduler Contact: 4-8 hours
- Session Scheduling: 24-72 hours
- Session Completion: 30-60 minutes
- Document Delivery: Within 48 hours
